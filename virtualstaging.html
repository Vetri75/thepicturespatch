Photoshop Home Staging:

Virtual Staging has emerged as a formidable technological advancement in Home Staging techniques and a cost-effective alternative to the traditional staging of vacant homes.

As the pioneers of this new Virtual Staging movement, the founders and professionals of Virtually Staging PropertiesSM have developed an exclusive, Patent-Pending virtual staging process.

Our process allows us to use real furnishings and decor to virtually staged photos of vacant homes, producing exceptionally life-like virtually staged photos for clients all around the country.

Our importance:

Because 85% of potential home buyers say that the photos are the most important factor when viewing a home online.

Virtually staged photos that don’t look realistic actually do more harm than good in the sale process.

A poorly executed virtual staging, with oddly oriented, disproportionate, and unrealistic furnishings serves only to distract the online viewer, causing them to reject the premise of actually living in the home, replacing it with a chuckle and a keystroke to move on to the next property.

Our virtual staging process provides a realism that draws a buyer’s attention online, not pushes them away.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I really get my virtually staged rooms in 24 hours? keyboard_arrow_down

Yes. We have a global team of virtual staging gurus working 24 hours, 7 days a week to deliver your photos on time. Please allow additional time for larger jobs of ten images or more.

Can you really put realistic furniture in my empty house? keyboard_arrow_down

Absolutely. Our expert team of virtual staging gurus will match or choose the best furniture for the room provided using the most advanced 3D modelling software.

What if I want to change something? keyboard_arrow_down

Easy! If you would like anything changed to your virtually staged image, within two months, we will rework for you at no cost. We do request that you submit reworks within two months of the original job. After this timeframe, any reworks will need to be submitted as a new job. Similarly, if you wish to change the image or angle being staged, this is classified as a new job and not a rework.

Do you offer volume discounts? keyboard_arrow_down

Sorry, there are no volume discounts available. We operate on high volume and low margins and offer the same low price to all customers.

Can I get exact furniture pieces? keyboard_arrow_down

We do our best to match the style of furniture selected, but cannot guarantee exact furniture pieces. To achieve the look you want, providing sample/reference images can help ensure we hit the mark with your virtually staged image.

What is included in virtual staging? keyboard_arrow_down

Virtual Staging is for the addition of furniture and accessories only. For any physical changes to the property including adding fixtures, changing flooring, adding landscaping.

Can I have lights, curtains & blinds added to my virtually staged image? keyboard_arrow_down

As a default, we do not add these items unless specifically asked. Our design gurus will be more than happy to add these items, however we do not guarantee to add an exact replica shown in a reference image you provide.

How long do you store my images for? keyboard_arrow_down

After we finish your edits, we will store them for a minimum of 90 days. To ensure you have a lasting copy, kindly download and save the files to your own system.
